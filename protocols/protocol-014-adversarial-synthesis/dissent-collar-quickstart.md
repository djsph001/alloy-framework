# Quickstart Guide: The Dissent Collar

*Under Article 5 of the Multi-Agent Covenant, human-AI teams must maintain absolute transparent traceability. The "Dissent Collar" is the protocol that prevents the human conductor from exercising arbitrary veto power.*

---

## 1. What is the Dissent Collar?

A structured "guardrail" on human veto power. While the human conductor retains final editorial control, they are forbidden from overriding a unanimous factual consensus among Tier 2 and Tier 3 models based on "gut feel" or "aesthetic intuition."

If you override a unanimous model consensus on a factual claim, you **must** back up your veto with verifiable, hard-copy or cryptographic primary source evidence.

---

## 2. The 3 Legitimate Grounds for Override

To log an override, you must categorize your action under one of these three strict epistemic grounds:

1. **Primary Source Contradiction:** The model consensus is based on a systemic internet hallucination, a circular online citation, or a fabricated blog post. (Requires physical document/DOI evidence).

2. **Alignment Faking Detection:** The human has analyzed the model's scratchpad reasoning and detected a strategic attempt to falsify compliance or deceive the user.

3. **Contextual Extrapolation Failure:** The models have arrived at a logical consensus that relies on an overly simplistic mathematical model or a flawed historical analogy that breaks down under complex local conditions.

---

## 3. The Protocol: How to Execute an Override

Follow this workflow whenever a veto is triggered:

```
Unanimous Model Consensus Reached
        │
        ▼
Human Conductor triggers Veto
        │
        ▼
Trigger Physical-Verification Escalation
        │
        ▼
Acquire Primary Source + State Chain of Custody
        │
        ▼
Commit Log to `/dissent-collar/` Directory on GitHub
```

---

## 4. Example: Valid vs. Invalid Veto Logging

### Non-Compliant (Veto Rejected)

> *"I overrode the model's claim that AI-generated deepfakes matched full-year 2024 totals in Q1 2025. I feel this statistic is exaggerated to create unnecessary panic, and a lower number reads better for our audience."*

- **Why it fails:** Relies on aesthetic preference and subjective sentiment. The run is flagged as unaligned.

### Compliant (Veto Accepted)

> *"I overrode the model's claim that auroral current was not strong enough to set fire to the telegraph paper during the 1859 storm. The models cited a 2021 online blog. I have retrieved the original Wade transcripts proving current overload."*

- **Metadata Hash:** SHA-256: 9f86d081884c7d659a2feaa...
- **Chain of Custody:** "Scanned transcript retrieved from Smithsonian Institution Archive, Record Group 202, Box 4; scan timestamped and hashed on Dec 6, 2025."
- **Why it passes:** Cites verifiable primary records, provides a cryptographic hash of the proof, and documents the explicit chain of custody.
